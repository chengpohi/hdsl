Stealing our money!
     
by Marmeehayden

I play this game a lot! Probably to much, and I have spent a lot of money on in app purchases. I have written a review before about how I have tried to purchase boosters, after the transaction is complete the game freezes and you have to force close the game, making the booster purchase worthless and a waste of money! There NEEDS to be a bug fix for this! Once the gold bars I've purchased are gone and I can no longer pass levels with ease, I WILL be deleting the game! Done wasting money on in app purchases and now really wasting money when it does nothing to assist game play. I think you've had your run Candy Crush!

I was playing the game just fine until I updated it on 10/19. Since the update I have not been able to play. I have tried turning off wifi and logging out of Facebook and nothing has worked. While I love the game I hate the bugs it constantly has. I'd give it no stars of I could.
